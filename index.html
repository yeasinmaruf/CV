I am Mohammad Yeasin Maruf, the founder and the CEO of the company called The IdeaMan. In our company we basically work with new ideas, do research on them and find out effective solutions to work with different kind of ideas. We work on business ideas as well as development ideas. We also help people to create their own ideas. The idea of founding such a company came to my mind when I was a student of North South University. One day I was working on a business project report and found that a lot of people are having problem in creating ideas. Many people have enough resources to start their own business but they don't have an effective and at the same time creative ideas. That time the idea of my company came into my mind and I found that I can help businesses with my ideas to become successful and to grow at the same time.
